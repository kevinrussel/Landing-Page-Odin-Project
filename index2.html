About Goose-Trade

Goose-Trade is a second hand market designed to provide a safe and secure platform where students can buy and sell goods. It aims to foster a community of trust and sustainability, encouraging students to recycle and reuse items, reduce waste, and save money. Join Goose-Trade today and discover great deals from fellow students.

Why Choose Goose-Trade?

Sustainability:

Sustainability is at the heart of our mission, as buying and selling second-hand goods reduces waste and promotes a circular economy.

Locally Owned:

Our local roots allow us to offer personalized assistance, fair prices, and a trustworthy platform for all your pre-owned item needs.

Security:

We ensure a seamless experience when buying and selling second-hand goods by not keeping any private information and offering a trustworthy platform for all your pre-owned item needs.
